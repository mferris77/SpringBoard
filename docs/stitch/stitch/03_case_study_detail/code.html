The Global Logistics Reset

Automating cross-Atlantic supply chain reporting for 95% efficiency gains and recovering 4,200 annual hours.

history_edu

The Manual Burden

Prior to Q3 2023, the Logistics Reconciliation process was a monolith of manual labor. Every Monday morning, six senior analysts across three time zones would log into SAP, export 14 distinct CSV files, and begin the arduous process of "stitching" them together.

The friction was palpable. Data formatting inconsistencies led to broken VLOOKUPs. Email chains for clarification stretched into Tuesday afternoons. By the time the report reached the Executive Leadership Team on Wednesday, the data was already 48 hours stale.

"We were spending 80% of our time cleaning data and only 20% actually analyzing what it meant for the client."

This workflow consumed approximately 85 collective hours per week, creating a bottleneck that prevented the team from taking on higher-value strategic consulting work. The error rate hovered around 12%, necessitating frequent restatements.

The Automation Engine

The solution deployed is a Python-based ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipeline hosted on the internal Kantar cloud. It triggers automatically at 4:00 AM GMT every Monday.

Using the pandas library, the script connects directly to the SAP data warehouse, bypassing the CSV export step entirely. It applies 45 distinct logic rules to clean, normalize, and merge the datasets in memory. 

def orchestrate_pipeline():
    start_time = time.time()
    
    # 1. Extraction
    raw_data = sap_connector.fetch_batch(BATCH_ID)
    
    # 2. Transformation
    clean_data = apply_cleaning_rules(raw_data)
    merged_view = merge_datasets(clean_data)
    
    # 3. Load to Tableau
    tableau_pusher.upload(merged_view)
    
    return f"Completed in {time.time() - start_time} seconds"

The result is a fully refreshed Tableau dashboard ready for review by 8:00 AM Monday. The total runtime has been reduced from 3 days to approximately 4 minutes.
