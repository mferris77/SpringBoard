# Kantar - Company Context

## What is Kantar?
Kantar is a global market research company headquartered in London, UK. They specialize in:
- **Brand equity studies** - tracking 4.3 million consumers across 21,000+ brands
- **Consumer behavior analysis** - understanding what people buy and why
- **Media effectiveness** - measuring advertising impact
- **Market trends** - industry analysis and forecasting
